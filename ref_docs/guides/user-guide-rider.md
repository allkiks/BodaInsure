## 1. Overview

### What is BodaInsure?

BodaInsure is a digital insurance platform that allows you to get Third-Party Only (TPO) insurance for your bodaboda through affordable daily payments.

### Why BodaInsure?

- **Affordable**: Pay 87 KES daily instead of 3,500 KES at once
- **Convenient**: Register and pay using your phone
- **Fast**: Get your policy within 6 hours of completing requirements
- **Legal**: Stay compliant with Kenyan traffic laws

### Payment Model

| Step | Amount | What You Get |
|------|--------|--------------|
| 1. Initial Deposit | 1,048 KES | 1-month policy issued |
| 2. Daily Payments | 87 KES x 30 days | 11-month policy issued |
| **Total** | **3,658 KES** | **Full year coverage** |

## 2. Getting Started

### Requirements

Before you start, ensure you have:

- **Mobile Phone** (Android smartphone recommended, but feature phones work via USSD)
- **M-Pesa Account** (registered and active)
- **National ID Card** (both front and back)
- **Driving License** (valid motorcycle license)
- **Motorcycle Logbook** (or registration certificate)
- **KRA PIN Certificate** (if available)

### Download the App

**For Smartphone Users:**
1. Open Google Play Store (Android) or App Store (iOS)
2. Search for "BodaInsure"
3. Download and install the app

**For Feature Phone Users:**
- Use USSD by dialing `*384*123#` (see [USSD Guide](#7-using-ussd))

## 3. Registration Process

### Step 1: Enter Your Phone Number

1. Open the BodaInsure app
2. Enter your phone number (format: 07XX XXX XXX or 01XX XXX XXX)
3. Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
4. Tap **"Continue"**

### Step 2: Verify Your Phone (OTP)

1. You will receive an SMS with a 6-digit code
2. Enter the code in the app
3. The code expires in 5 minutes - request a new one if needed
4. Maximum 3 OTP requests per hour allowed

### Step 3: Complete Registration

Once verified, your account is created. You can now proceed to:
- Upload KYC documents
- Make your first payment

### Important Notes

- Your phone number is your unique identifier
- Keep your phone secure - it's linked to your insurance account
- You can register only once per phone number

## 4. KYC Document Upload

### Required Documents

| Document | Requirements |
|----------|--------------|
| **National ID (Front)** | Clear photo showing your face and ID number |
| **National ID (Back)** | Clear photo showing full back of ID |
| **Driving License** | Valid motorcycle license (Class A) |
| **Motorcycle Logbook** | Registration document or logbook |
| **KRA PIN Certificate** | Optional but recommended |
| **Passport Photo** | Clear photo of your face |

### How to Upload Documents

1. Go to **"KYC"** or **"Documents"** section in the app
2. Select the document type
3. Take a clear photo or select from gallery
4. Ensure all text is readable
5. Submit for review

### Tips for Good Photos

- Use good lighting (natural light is best)
- Hold the camera steady (avoid blur)
- Ensure all corners of the document are visible
- Make sure text is readable
- Avoid shadows covering important information

### Document Review

- Documents are reviewed within 24-48 hours
- You will receive SMS notification of approval or rejection
- If rejected, you can re-upload with corrections

### KYC Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Documents not yet uploaded |
| **In Review** | Documents submitted, under review |
| **Approved** | All documents verified |
| **Rejected** | Documents rejected - re-upload required |
| **Incomplete** | Some documents missing |

## 5. Making Payments

### Initial Deposit (1,048 KES)

This is your first payment that activates your insurance.

**To Pay:**
1. Open the BodaInsure app
2. Go to **"Payments"** or **"Pay"**
3. Select **"Initial Deposit"**
4. Confirm the amount (1,048 KES)
5. Tap **"Pay with M-Pesa"**
6. You will receive M-Pesa STK Push on your phone
7. Enter your M-Pesa PIN to complete payment
8. Wait for confirmation

**After Payment:**
- Your 1-month policy will be issued within 6 hours
- You will receive the policy via WhatsApp and SMS
- You can download the policy PDF from the app

### Daily Payments (87 KES)

After your initial deposit, make 30 daily payments to get your 11-month policy.

**To Pay:**
1. Open the app
2. Go to **"Payments"**
3. Select **"Daily Payment"**
4. You can pay for multiple days at once
5. Complete M-Pesa payment as above

### Payment Methods

| Method | How to Use |
|--------|------------|
| **M-Pesa STK Push** | Payment prompt sent to your phone (recommended) |
| **USSD** | Dial `*384*123#` and select payment option |

### Payment Schedule

- Daily payments can be made at any time
- Set up payment reminders in the app settings
- Receive SMS reminders before payment is due

### Grace Period

- If you miss payments, you have a **7-day grace period**
- After grace period, your policy may lapse
- Make missed payments as soon as possible

## 6. Viewing Your Policy

### In the App

1. Open the BodaInsure app
2. Go to **"Policy"** or **"My Policy"**
3. View your current policy status and details

### Policy Information Displayed

- **Policy Number**: Your unique policy identifier
- **Status**: Active, Pending, Expired, etc.
- **Start Date**: When coverage begins
- **End Date**: When coverage expires
- **Vehicle Details**: Registered motorcycle information

### Download Policy PDF

1. Go to **"Policy"** section
2. Tap **"Download PDF"**
3. Save to your phone
4. Print or show to traffic police when needed

### Policy Delivery

Your policy document is delivered via:
- **WhatsApp** (preferred)
- **SMS** with download link
- **App** download

## 7. Using USSD

For riders without smartphones, all core features are accessible via USSD.

### Access USSD

Dial: `*384*123#`

### USSD Menu

```
Welcome to BodaInsure
1. Check Balance
2. Make Payment
3. View Policy Status
4. Help
0. Exit
```

### Check Balance

1. Dial `*384*123#`
2. Select option **1**
3. View your wallet balance and days paid

### Make Payment

1. Dial `*384*123#`
2. Select option **2**
3. Choose payment type:
   - Initial Deposit (1,048 KES)
   - Daily Payment (87 KES)
   - Multiple Days
4. Confirm amount
5. Complete M-Pesa payment

### View Policy Status

1. Dial `*384*123#`
2. Select option **3**
3. View:
   - Policy number
   - Status (Active/Pending/Expired)
   - Days remaining

### USSD Tips

- Sessions timeout after 180 seconds
- Keep your responses quick
- Save the shortcode in your contacts

## 9. Frequently Asked Questions

### Registration

**Q: Can I register with two phone numbers?**
A: No, each phone number can only have one account.

**Q: What if I entered the wrong phone number?**
A: Contact support to correct your information before completing registration.

**Q: Can I change my phone number later?**
A: Yes, contact support with your National ID for verification.

### Payments

**Q: Can I pay more than 87 KES at once?**
A: Yes! You can pay for multiple days in advance.

**Q: What happens if I miss a payment?**
A: You have a 7-day grace period. After that, your policy may lapse.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Yes, within 30 days you can cancel for a full refund (free-look period).

**Q: What if M-Pesa fails?**
A: Wait a few minutes and try again. If money was deducted, contact support.

### Policy

**Q: When will I receive my policy?**
A: Within 6 hours of meeting all requirements (KYC approved + payment confirmed).

**Q: Is my policy valid for traffic police?**
A: Yes! Show the PDF on your phone or print a copy.

**Q: Can I insure multiple motorcycles?**
A: Yes, but each motorcycle needs a separate registration.

**Q: What does TPO insurance cover?**
A: Third-Party Only covers damage or injury you cause to others. It does NOT cover damage to your own motorcycle.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app won't open. What do I do?**
A: Clear the app cache, or uninstall and reinstall. Your account data is safe.

**Q: I didn't receive my OTP. What should I do?**
A: Wait 60 seconds and request again. Check your SMS inbox (not spam).

**Q: The USSD session timed out.**
A: This happens after 180 seconds of inactivity. Simply dial again.
